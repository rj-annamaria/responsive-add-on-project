About VV

For nearly 17 years Ville Valo was known as the frontman of the Finnish hard rock band HIM. That changed in 2017, when HIM called it quits. Valo had collaborated with a number of other artists and bands during his group’s run but launched his own solo career — under the moniker VV — with the album “Neon Noir.” Valo wrote and produced all the instrument son the album’s 12 tracks, and the set debuted at No. 1 in his homeland. He’s now on the road promoting it, offering something new from an old friend in the music world…
